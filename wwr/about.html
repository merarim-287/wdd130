History

White water Rafting was cofounder by Jhon Doe in the year 1965, and was momentarily closed in the year 1985 for two years, and again in 2018 due to the phandemic. While it reopened again, it did with new safety precausions, as safety is our number one mission during all the trips. With cero fatalities and an increase number of new happy costumers, White Water Rafting keeps improving.

While Water Rafting has been open on and off, it has never stop improving, both safety and fun. It all started with a small crew of 5 employees who were actually the cofounders of the company, it was later doubled by 10 members. And so on throughout the years, until what it is now a crew of 49 employees with capacitations, training and certificates for safety and cpr.
